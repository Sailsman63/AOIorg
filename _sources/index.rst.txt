.. _intro:

   Art of Illusion is a free, open source 3D modelling and rendering studio.
   Many of its capabilities rival those found in commercial programs. Highlights
   include subdivision surface based modelling tools, skeleton based animation,
   and a graphical language for designing procedural textures and materials.


.. _platforms:

Cross-Platform:
###############

Art Of Illusion will run on any computer system that supports Java 8 or
higher.
